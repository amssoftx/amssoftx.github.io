Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1 Introduction

At AMSSoftX, we are committed to maintaining the trust and confidence of our visitors and clients. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when using our website or engaging our professional services.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiable Information: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, and company details provided via contact forms or project inquiry sessions.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and device information gathered via standard analytics tools to optimize our website performance.
  • Project Data: Sensitive technical specifications, hardware requirements, and software architecture details shared during the development lifecycle.

3 How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To communicate project status updates and technical documentation.
  • To personalize your experience and provide tailored technical support.
  • To fulfill legal obligations and process secure transactions.
  • To improve our AI models and internal coding tools (only with explicit anonymized consent).

4 Data Protection & Sharing

AMSSoftX utilizes enterprise-grade encryption for all data storage. We do not sell, rent, or lease your data to third parties. Information is only shared with trusted partners (like courier services or payment gateways) solely for the purpose of fulfilling your orders.
