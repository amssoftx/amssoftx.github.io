Hardware Shipping Policy

1. Production & Testing

AMSSoftX believes in quality over speed. Every custom IoT device, sensor module, or embedded hardware prototype undergoes a minimum of 48 hours of stress testing before it is approved for shipping.

2. Shipping Timelines

Once your order has passed quality assurance, it is dispatched via premium courier partners. While we aim for delivery within 7-10 business days for most domestic regions, please note that customs or logistics delays are outside our control.

3. Damaged or Lost Shipments

We take extreme care in packaging hardware to prevent electrostatic or physical damage. In the unlikely event that your hardware arrives in a non-functional state:

  • You must document the condition of the box and the product immediately upon delivery.
  • You are required to submit an unboxing video or clear photographic evidence to our support email within 48 hours.
  • Replacement units will be shipped following our assessment of the damage report.
